Renewables in Parallel Universes

Using old weather forecasts for a fresh perspective on renewable energy.

In my PhD work, I curated a set of past ECMWF ensemble weather predictions to repurpose them as a dataset for evaluating renewable power systems. By doing so, future installations can be assessed not just on the weather that has happened, but also on the weather that could have happened, offering a more comprehensive insight into their potential reliability.
